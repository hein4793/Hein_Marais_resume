About Me

Welcome to my online portfolio. I've created this site as a means to portray my expertise and market myself to future employers.

I've picked up some critical developer skills and languages, that have enabled me to build websites, not via template, but rather the way I want them. Mastering the MERN stack, I am now able to develop online websites using Javascript, HTML, CSS, MongoDB, Node, Express, React and more. My work is documented on my Github account. You're highly encouraged to check that out. My passion for Marketing practices and content creation allows me to see the world differently. I don't build websites as a developer but rather as a consumer. I build what your clients want.

Striving to go the extra mile, I work efficiently in a team environment while always taking initiative. As an avid learner, I use the latest technology to produce websites that allow for the integration of advertising and marketing solutions that work. My IT interests are ever-expanding which allows me to draw valuable experience from my day to day business and implement lessons learned in a way that benefits my work. Give me a try, I'll go above and beyond to exceed all expectations.
